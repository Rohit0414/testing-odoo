Enterprise-Grade Features

⚙

Bidirectional Part & BOM Sync

Synchronize WTParts from Windchill to create/update Odoo Products and manage multi-level Bills of Materials with precision. Ensure manufacturing BOMs are always aligned with the engineering design.

🔄

Lifecycle & Revision Control

Respect and transfer product lifecycle states and revision information from Windchill to Odoo, providing clear visibility on the status of parts for procurement and manufacturing teams.

📄

Document & CAD Data Linkage

Automatically attach viewables (like PDFs and STEP files) or link directly to CAD documents in Windchill from the corresponding product form in Odoo. Give shop floor and service teams access to the right visuals.

🛡

Flexible & Extensible

Built on a robust framework, the connector can be extended to handle custom attributes, different Windchill object types, and complex business logic to perfectly fit your unique processes.

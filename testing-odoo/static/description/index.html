FAQ – NEW

Questions about Token Meter & Presets

How do you calculate tokens and cost?

We display prompt and completion tokens reported by the model. Estimated cost is computed from your selected model’s public pricing. Final billing is handled by the provider.

Can I hide token badges for end users?

Yes. Admins can toggle visibility globally or restrict to admin users in Settings → Niyu AI Analyst.

Can I customize the Predefined Questions?

Absolutely. Add, edit, or disable presets per module to match your KPIs and custom fields.

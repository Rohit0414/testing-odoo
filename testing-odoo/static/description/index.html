Odoo 17 and later. Check the module listing for the latest compatibility.

How is my data kept secure?

Your business data never leaves your environment. Only schema (table/field names) and your question go to OpenAI via your key; SQL executes in your Odoo DB.

How do you calculate tokens and cost?

We display prompt + completion tokens reported by the model. Estimated cost is based on your selected model‚Äôs public pricing; final billing is by OpenAI.

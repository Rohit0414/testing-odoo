Your Questions, Answered

Which HR and Knowledge systems can it integrate with?

Our platform supports a wide array of systems out-of-the-box, including Workday, BambooHR, Confluence, SharePoint, and more. We also offer a flexible API for connecting to custom-built internal systems.

How does it ensure the privacy of employee data from the HR system?

The Assistant operates on a "least privilege" principle. It only accesses the minimum data required to answer a query (e.g., name, title, department) and strictly adheres to the role-based access controls you have defined in your HR system.

Can we customize the AI's personality or response style?

Yes. The Assistant can be configured to match your company's tone of voice, whether that's formal and professional or friendly and conversational, ensuring a consistent brand experience for your employees.

What does the implementation process involve?

Our dedicated onboarding team guides you through a simple, three-step process: Connect your data sources, configure your access controls, and deploy to your users. Most organizations are up and running in a matter of days, not months.

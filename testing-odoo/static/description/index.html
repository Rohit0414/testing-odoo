Windchill Connector

Seamlessly Integrate Your Odoo Environment with PTC Windchill. The bridge between your business and engineering data.

Unlock Your Engineering Data

The Windchill Connector provides a robust, bidirectional integration, allowing for the smooth exchange of vital product data between Odoo and PTC Windchill. Eliminate manual data entry, reduce errors, and accelerate your product lifecycle.

Core Features

Part and BOM Synchronization

Synchronize parts, materials, and Bills of Materials (BOMs) between Windchill and Odoo to ensure consistency across your enterprise.

Document and CAD File Exchange

Seamlessly transfer documents, drawings, and CAD files from Windchill directly to your Odoo records.

Change Management Integration

Integrate Engineering Change Notices (ECNs) from Windchill into your Odoo workflows for streamlined change management processes.

Get Started in Minutes

Connecting Odoo and Windchill is a straightforward process. Follow these simple steps to get up and running.

  1. Install the Windchill Connector module in your Odoo instance.
  2. Configure the connection settings with your Windchill server details.
  3. Map the relevant Odoo and Windchill fields.
  4. Start synchronizing your data.

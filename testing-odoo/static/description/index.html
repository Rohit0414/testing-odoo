Frequently Asked Questions

What content sources can it connect to?

The eLearning Assistant is built with flexibility in mind and can connect to a wide range of popular knowledge management systems, including Confluence, SharePoint, internal websites, and document repositories. Contact us for specific integration questions.

How does it handle complex, multi-part questions?

Our advanced AI models can deconstruct complex queries into smaller parts, find the relevant information for each, and then synthesize a comprehensive answer that addresses all aspects of your original question.

What is the setup process like?

Setup is straightforward. It involves securely authorizing the Assistant to access your chosen knowledge sources in a read-only capacity. Our support team works with you to ensure a smooth and secure integration process, typically completed within hours.

Does it respect user permissions?

Yes. The Assistant can be configured to respect existing user permissions. This means users will only receive answers from content they are already authorized to view, ensuring data confidentiality is maintained.
